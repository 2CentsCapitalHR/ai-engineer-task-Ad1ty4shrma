Post-Registration Services: Letters and Permits

The ADGM Registration Authority provides various post-registration services to assist companies with their ongoing operational needs.

Types of Letters and Certificates Available:
- Certificate of Good Standing: Confirms that a company is duly incorporated and in compliance with its filing obligations.
- Certificate of Incumbency: Lists the current directors and officers of the company.
- Visa-Related Letters: Letters required for immigration or visa application purposes, often referred to as a "Letter of No Objection".
- Other Official Letters: Various other letters can be requested for official purposes, such as bank account opening or dealing with other government entities.

Application Process:
Requests for these letters and permits are typically made through the ADGM online portal. A fee may be applicable for the issuance of these documents. The portal provides a list of available services and the requirements for each request.
